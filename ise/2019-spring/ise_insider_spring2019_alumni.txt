News from the Department of Industrial and Systems Engineering at the University of Florida

Welcome to the Department of Industrial & Systems Engineering at the University of Florida's digital newsletter. Each semester, you will receive a message from us highlighting departmental updates and successes. We look forward to connecting with you!

Message from Chair:
We are pleased to share with you our Spring 2019 electronic newsletter.: e.g.: https://www.ise.ufl.edu/blog/2019/04/ise-insider-spring-2019-message-from-the-chair/

Research Spotlight:
Dr. Hongcheng Liu discusses his current research focus on personalized radiation therapy. e.g.: https://www.ise.ufl.edu/blog/2019/04/research-spotlight-dr-hongcheng-liu/

Faculty Spotlight:
Dr. Panos Pardalos received the Humboldt Research Award from the Alexander von Humboldt Foundation. e.g.: https://www.ise.ufl.edu/blog/2019/03/panos-pardalos-receives-humboldt-research-award/

Alumni Spotlight:
UF ISE Alumna Linda Parker Hudson has been elected to the National Academy of Engineering. e.g.: https://www.eng.ufl.edu/newengineer/news/uf-alumna-linda-parker-hudson-elected-to-national-academy-of-engineering/

Student Spotlight:
Two students receive scholarships from the Association of American-Cuban Engineers. e.g.: https://www.ise.ufl.edu/blog/2019/03/two-ise-students-receive-scholarships-from-the-association-of-cuban-american-engineers/

Follow us on Instagram:
Stay informed on what's going on in the department by following us on Instagram. e.g.: https://www.instagram.com/uf_ise/

UF ISE Graduate Program Ranks #12 Among Public Institutions:
The Department of Industrial & Systems Engineering (ISE) at the University of Florida has moved up two spots in the rankings for public institution graduate schools. e.g.: https://www.ise.ufl.edu/blog/2019/03/uf-ise-graduate-program-ranks-no-12-among-public-institutions/
