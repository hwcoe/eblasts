Gator Engineering Innovation & Reunion Weekend

September 19-21, 2013
Presented by the UF Engineering Innovation Institute

Enjoy an orange-and-blue weekend including the Gator Engineering Innovation Summit, an evening at the Samuel P. Harn Museum of Art, and our Alumni Tailgate Reunion prior to the UF vs. Tennessee football game.

Innovation Summit

Thursday & Friday, Sept. 19 & 20
J. Wayne Reitz Union

It’s time to Unleash the Creative Spirit of the Gator Engineer! At this year’s summit, thought leaders, industry giants and successful alums will discuss the role of creativity in entrepreneurship and innovation. Learn how to Think Big, Win Big, and change the the way we play and live.

Ignite! at the Harn

Friday, Sept. 20
Samuel P. Harn Museum of Art

Come check out our very own Gator Engineering startup companies, student entrepreneurs and innovators as we host a reception at the Samuel P. Harn Museum of Art. Nationally recognized Gainesville startups Fracture, Feathr, Altavian and many others will be on hand to demonstrate their products and explain how they got their start with Gator Engineering.

Tailgate Reunion

Saturday, Sept. 21
J. Wayne Reitz Union North Lawn

Enjoy a fun and festive orange-and-blue tailgate party with other Gator Engineering alumni, friends and faculty. Come interact with members of our student organizations as they display their latest projects and initiatives. Find out why they are among the highest ranked in the nation when it comes to winning competitions and garnering recognition. Don’t miss face painting, music and food – all before you head over to the Swamp to watch our Gators take on the Tennessee Vols!
