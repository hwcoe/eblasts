THE GATOR NATION IS EVERYWHERE


Gator Engineers work, live and shape society in nearly every industry. More than 50,000 alumni from the UF Herbert Wertheim College of Engineering (like you!) are transforming the future – and on November 10, we’re celebrating our legacy and our future back at The Swamp.

Join fellow Gator Engineers at our Alumni Reunion on November 10. As a special to Spring 2018 Graduates - tell us where you are now, and you’ve got a chance to win a pair of complimentary entry tickets to this year’s Alumni Reunion, plus tickets to the Florida vs. South Carolina game!


ALUMNI REUNION GIVEAWAY DETAILS:

  • Who can enter? If you graduated with a Bachelor, Master or Doctoral engineering degree from UF in Spring 2018, you’re eligible. (Only one entry per graduate is allowed, duplicates will be deleted.)
  • What are the prizes? Each winner will receive two (2) complimentary entry tickets (meal included) to the UF Herbert Wertheim College of Engineering’s Alumni Reunion on November 10, 2018. Each winner will also receive two (2) tickets to the Florida Gators vs. South Carolina Gamecocks football game on November 10, 2018; seats will be located in the college’s group block.
  • How many will win? Three (3) graduates will be randomly selected to win.
  • When will the winners be announced? The winning graduates will be notified on or before October 10, 2018. The winners must attend the reunion and football game (no substitutions allowed) and must present a government-issued ID in order to claim the prizes. (No reselling, exchanges, redistribution and/or refunds of any tickets are allowed.)
  • What’s the catch? None! We just want you at our Alumni Reunion. (But please keep in mind winners and guests are responsible for their own travel and accommodations booking and expenses).
