Greetings, Gator Engineers!

October is National Cybersecurity Awareness Month and UF's cybersecurity experts are available to answer your questions about online safety, mobile security, cloud storage, cyberterrorism, anything cyber-related!

On Tuesday, October 28, UF’s College of Engineering and College of Journalism and Communications will co-host a 90-minute livestream event. Three engineering professors – all recently hired under UF’s Preeminence Plan – will answer questions and reveal their new center: SouthEasterN Security for Enterprise and Infrastructure (SENSEI).

Experts from another preeminence research area, STEM Translational Communication Research, will be evaluating participation and handing out prizes.

Post questions from now until October 27 using the hashtag #askSENSEI on Twitter (@FloridaEngineer), or the Facebook page for the event. Post early and you could be selected to attend the livestream event in person.

Tune in October 28, 2014 from 12:30-2pm EDT at http://www.eng.ufl.edu/cybersecurity to hear the experts answer your questions.
