PLEASE JOIN US FOR AN MCLE EVENT

CYBERSECURITY IN THE NEW NORMAL: THREATS, TRENDS AND TIPS FOR TODAY’S BUSINESS LEADERS

Please join DLA Piper, the University of Florida College of Engineering and Cyxtera for a discussion featuring Senator Saxby Chambliss, DLA Piper partner, former Vice Chairman of the Senate Select Committee on Intelligence, and former chairman of the House Intelligence Subcommittee on Terrorism and Homeland Security.

The new normal for business today means an increasingly digital business landscape subject to sophisticated cyberthreats and heightened regulatory demands, both domestically and abroad. Business leaders who understand this evolving landscape are no longer luxuries in the board room, but necessities for conducting day-to-day business and responding to potentially catastrophic cyberincidents.

Join us for an evening of discussions with prominent professionals from politics, law, academia and information technology as we discuss cyberthreat vectors and risk mitigation trends and offer effective compliance tips for today’s business leaders.

MCLE credit is available for this event.
