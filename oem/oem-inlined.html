UF's Outreach Engineering Management Program provides technically degreed students the opportunity to obtain an engineering master's from a highly ranked university, without experiencing a career interruption. The infrequency of class meetings makes it possible for out-of-state students to participate, and provides an excellent alternative to an online degree program.

Fellow students have outstanding academic qualifications and represent top-notch employers. Experience lively classroom discussions, team collaborations, and networking opportunities. Many employers will pay a portion of the course fees.

OEM awards a Master of Science in Industrial & Systems Engineering (Engineering Management track). The blended curriculum applies to a broad range of disciplines and industries, and thus benefits technical professionals with almost any engineering or STEM background.

Information sessions and open houses are available throughout Florida and online.
