Make the Match with UF ChE! Attention: ExxonMobil Matching Gift Program Changes on Oct. 1, 2019 ExxonMobil & Gator Chemical Engineers Make A Great Match! Remember all of the opportunities granted to you as a Gator Engineer? Experiential learning shapes the very fabric of our students’ education at UF and Chemical Engineering (ChE). The presence of UF ChE students on a national and global stage elevates the department and university, while offering our students the opportunity to grow professionally and to be recognized by their peers. Whether it’s presenting at conferences or competing as a team, UF ChE is committed to the support and sponsorship of these students and we ask for your commitment too. Impact a student’s life today as someone made a difference for you. The ExxonMobil matching gift program for all employees changes from a 3:1 match to a to a 2:1 match on Oct. 1, 2019. Retiree matches also change from a 3:1 to a 1:1 match on Oct. 1, 2019. The annual limit of $7,500 remains unchanged. The opportunity to make an impact on the world is what it truly means to be a Gator Engineer!
