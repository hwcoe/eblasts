MARCH 8, 2016 • J. WAYNE REITZ UNION
The Herbert Wertheim College of Engineering proudly hosts the Committee of 200 Reachout

The Herbert Wertheim College of Engineering is a national leader in advancing women in engineering—from students earning bachelor’s and doctoral degrees to distinguished faculty working on bold innovations.

Join the preeminent women of The Committee of 200 (C200) as they engage students, alumni, faculty and friends in a discussion about leadership, STEM education, and transforming our future as women.
Register Now
Speakers include:

Linda Hudson, UF Distinguished Alumnus and CEO, The Cardea Group
Tanya Fratto, Board Director, Boart Longyear
Shellye Archambeau, CEO, MetricStream

The Engineering Leadership Institute at the University of Florida hosts an annual summit to connect UF’s engineering alumni, faculty and students with world-renowned thought leaders—inspiring new ways of approaching and solving problems. This year’s summit is in collaboration with C200, an organization formed by the world’s most successful women entrepreneurs and corporate innovators.
Event rates are available at:

Hilton UF Conference Center
Residence Inn Gainesville I-75
