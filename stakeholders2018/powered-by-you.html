On March 23, 2018, Gator Engineering alumni and friends gathered at the Hilton UF Conference Center to celebrate 108 years of dedicated service, selfless mentorship and loyal support to the Herbert Wertheim College of Engineering at the University of Florida. 

These gifts of Time, Talent and Treasures help create numerous opportunities for our faculty and students to excel in their education, research and career. These New Engineers are transforming the future of health, energy, security, sustainability and more - and they are Powered by You. 

We would like to take this opportunity to thank all of our alumni and friends for your contributions and continued support.  We hope these highlights provide a glimpse of the impact your gifts have made to the college.
