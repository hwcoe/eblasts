You Are Cordially Invited to the UF Alumni and Friends Social at ANS 2019

Please join the University of Florida's Nuclear Engineering Program and the Department of Materials Science and Engineering for the 2019 American Nuclear Society Winter Meeting Alumni and Friends reception. This will be a great opportunity to mingle with NE and MSE faculty, students and fellow alumni over some light refreshments. We also encourage you to bring up to two guests to join in the fun.

We look forward to seeing you on Monday, November 18 in Washington, D.C.!

GO GATORS!
