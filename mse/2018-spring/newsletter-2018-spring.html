In 1959, Dr. Fred Rhines established the Department of Materials Science and Engineering at the University of Florida. In the nearly 60 years since its founding, the department has grown to include more than 30 faculty and nearly 550 students. We rank among the Top 10 materials science and engineering departments at U.S. public universities, and the department is looking to become the top program with the help of our faculty and alumni. In 2011, the department added to its research competency by becoming the home of the Nuclear Engineering Program, which has opened the door for even more opportunities for students. Each year our faculty, students and alumni garner many accomplishments, and the 1959 newsletter will bring you a selection of those success stories in each edition.
