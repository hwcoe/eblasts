Registration open for the 2019 Gator Engineering Alumni Reunion On Saturday, November 9, gather at the Reitz Union North Lawn for this year's Gator Engineering Alumni Reunion. Enjoy a tailgate celebration with your growing network of alumni from the Herbert Wertheim College of Engineering before heading to the FLORIDA VS. VANDERBILT football game. Tailgate begins three hours before kickoff. Attendees who purchase a tailgate ticket package will also receive a special college-branded giveaway. New this year: You will only be able to purchase tickets for the tailgate here. Once you complete your tailgate registration, you will receive a confirmation email with a link to Engineering’s football ticket block where you will be able to buy football tickets.
