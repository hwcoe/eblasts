Former UF Engineering dean Pramod Khargonekar named Vice Chancellor of Research at the University of California, Irvine

Khargonekar served as dean of the Herbert Wertheim College of Engineering from 2001 to 2009. He returned to teaching and research at UF as the Eckis Professor of Electrical and Computer Engineering, amid serving as assistant director of the NSF Engineering Directorate, which invests more than $900 million annually in frontier engineering research and education. David Hintenlang named Chief Medical Physicist at Ohio State

Biomedical professor David Hintenlang joined the radiology department at Ohio State University this fall as Chief Medical Physicist. IN MEMORIAM

Rudolf E. Kalman (1930 – 2016)

Famously known as the father of the Kalman filter, a mathematical algorithm that removes “noise” from streams of data to increase accuracy, Professor Emeritus Kalman was a mentor and friend to many members of the UF Engineering family.
Read more»

Arthur Saarinen (1928 – 2016)

The UF Distinguished Alumnus was respected around the world for his environmental engineering designs and ethics. Saarinen generously supported the college and served in various capacities, including the Dean’s Advisory Board. After he retired, Saarinen was invited to return to UF to teach classes in ethics.
