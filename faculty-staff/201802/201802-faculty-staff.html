COLLEGE NEWS

College Gains New Executive Director of Advancement

Ann Powers joined the college as the Executive Director of Advancement this summer. She came to us with over 34 years of experience in university advancement, most recently from the Carnegie Mellon University Tepper School of Business. Her background includes an extensive portfolio of principal gifts, leadership of a high performing fundraising team, and an in-depth knowledge of working with an engineering and technically related alumni base from a premier institution. She succeeds Meg Hendryx, who was promoted to the position of Assistant Vice President of Development at UF Advancement.

Discover CEO David Nelms Speaks to ELI students

David Nelms, Discover Finance CEO and UF MAE alumnus, visited campus to speak to Engineering Leadership Institute students. He met with ECE and CISE faculty in the Warren B. Nelms Institute for the Connected World to discuss the latest trends in the Internet of Things.

Human/Robot Team Gives Paraplegic Patients a Taste of Mobility

Warren Dixon, MAE, and his team, both human and robot, give paraplegic patients a taste of mobility and freedom. Dixon and UF startup MYOLYN are changing what it means to be disabled for the millions of people affected by neurological disorders.
