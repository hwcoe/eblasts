Toshi Nishida named new interim Associate Dean of Academic Affairs

Toshikazu Nishida, associate chair of research in ECE and affiliate professor in MAE, began his role as Interim Association Dean of Academic Affairs on July 15, 2017. Nishida serves as director of the MIST Center and co-founder of the Interdisciplinary Microsystems Group. As an integral member of the college leadership team, Nishida will oversee graduate education and academic faculty programs. Read more»

STAFF SUCCESS

Congratulations to our college Superior Accomplishment Award recipients

James Ogles, Karen J. Ehlers, Norman C. Green, Heather L. Nemeth, Jordan A. Williams, and Adrienne L. Cook were each selected for a 2017 Superior Accomplishment Award. The faculty and staff superior accomplishment awards program recognizes faculty and staff members who contribute outstanding and meritorious service in their fields. The program also recognizes those who have made exceptional contributions to UF’s efficiency and or quality of life to students and employees.
