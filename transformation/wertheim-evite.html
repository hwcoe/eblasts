Dear Gator Engineering partner,

I am delighted to inform you that we are about to launch the greatest expansion in the 105-year history of the UF College of Engineering. This expansion will be catalyzed by a $50M gift from Dr. Herbert and Nicole Wertheim and the Dr. Herbert & Nicole Wertheim Family Foundation. Please join me, President Fuchs, Provost Glover and our very special guests, Herbie and Nicole, this Thursday, October 1st for a celebration and an announcement.

We would be honored to have you join us just outside Weil Hall, at the corner of Gale Lemerand Drive and Stadium Road, to participate in this momentous occasion. Please arrive at 9:45 a.m. to participate in a Gator Walk to welcome our special guests – with remarks and festivities to follow.

Please let us know if you can attend (and if you need any special accommodations for you and/or your staff) by emailing jenli@eng.ufl.edu.

Cammy R. Abernathy
Dean, UF College of Engineering
