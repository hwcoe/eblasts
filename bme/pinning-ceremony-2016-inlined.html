Join us in honoring the J. Crayton Pruitt Family Department of Biomedical Engineering class of 2016

Be a part of our 3rd annual heartwarming tradition and sponsor an undergraduate student's graduation pin.

These custom-designed pins recognize our graduating students for their achievements, and serve as a guiding reminder of excellence and research to improve human health. It is a symbol the class of 2016 will carry with them long after they walk across the stage, and one that YOU can help make possible!
“Congratulations! Your graduation pin was generously sponsored by Michael Masem”

For just $100, you will provide a student with their graduation pin. Sponsor one or multiple students!

Every pin you sponsor will include a note to the student with your name, class year (if alumni) and a personal message provided by you. Your tax-deductible donation will also be used to grow and expand this tradition and to help support student activities.
“It meant so much to me that someone cared enough to provide us with this special symbol, to remind us why we went into this profession and the responsibility that comes with it.”
–Courtney K., student

Our goal is to have every pin sponsored in the Class of 2016. Your participation will inspire these graduates so that they too might one day support this wonderful tradition. If you are interested in sponsoring a student this year, kindly respond by March 31.

Sponsor a Pin

As a pin sponsor, you are invited to attend the Pinning Ceremony on May 3, 2015. The event will begin at 4:00pm and will be held at the Hilton UF Conference Center Hotel. If you are interested in attending, please RSVP to mmasem@eng.ufl.edu.
