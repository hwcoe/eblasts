When you sponsor a graduation pin for the J. Crayton Pruitt Family Department of Biomedical Engineering, you join the tradition of supporting the next generation of engineers as they strive for excellence in their efforts to improve human health.

The graduation pin is a symbol that will stay with the Class of 2016 long after they walk across the stage, and you can help each student to have one. Each custom-designed pin that you give includes a personal note from you.
“It meant so much to me that someone cared enough to provide us with this special symbol, to remind us why we went into this profession and the responsibility that comes with it.”
–Courtney K., student

Our goal is to have every pin sponsored for the Class of 2016. Each one costs $100. This is a tax-deductible donation and there is no limit on how many students you may sponsor. Your participation will inspire these graduates to give back and support others as they advance in their careers. All sponsors are invited to attend the Pinning Ceremony on May 1, 2016. The event will begin at 1:00 p.m. and will be held at the Hilton UF Conference Center Hotel.
