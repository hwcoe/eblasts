Sarasota County Gators - Join us !
You are invited to a gathering of fellow Gators, community leaders and interested high school students and their families to learn more about the Innovation Station program. Innovation Station is the first engineering extension office of the Herbert Wertheim College of Engineering in Florida providing access to unique and high-value UF resources. It focuses on establishing and growing partnerships with the private, academic and public sectors in the region with the focus set clearly on growing the region's innovation economy.
Join us as we celebrate the one year anniversary of this unique program. Admission is free if pre-registered via EventBrite.
Wednesday, April 5, 2017 • 6:00 pm
Mission Valley Golf and Country Club • Nokomis, FL
Guests must register in advance.
Please register by March 31.
