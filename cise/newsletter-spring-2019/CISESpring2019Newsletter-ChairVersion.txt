UF CISE: Spring 2019 Newsletter

Welcome to the digital newsletter from the University of Florida Department of Computer & Information Science & Engineering (CISE). Each semester, you will receive an email highlighting department updates and successes. We look forward to connecting with you.

Visit our website: https://www.cise.ufl.edu/


COLLEGE AWARDS
Prabhat Mishra, Ph.D., a professor, was selected as a 2019 Herbert Wertheim College of Engineering Doctoral Dissertation Advisor/Mentoring Awardee. Read more: https://www.cise.ufl.edu/mishra-selected-as-a-doctoral-dissertation-advisor-mentoring-awardee-by-college/

GIFT FUNDS PROFESSORSHIPS
Kevin R. B. Butler, Ph.D., and Daisy Zhe Wang, Ph.D., received the Arnold and Lisa Goldberg Rising Star Professorships in Computer Science. Read more: https://www.cise.ufl.edu/uf-cise-receives-1-million-gift-to-fund-two-rising-star-professorships/

ACM FELLOW
Juan E. Gilbert, Ph.D., the Banks Family Preeminence Endowed Professor and department chair, was named an ACM Fellow. Read more: https://www.cise.ufl.edu/gilbert-named-an-acm-fellow/

IEEE FELLOW
Ahmed Helmy, Ph.D., professor and graduate coordinator, has been named an IEEE Fellow. Read more: https://www.cise.ufl.edu/helmy-named-2019-ieee-fellow/

STUDENT AWARDS
Four women receive the Herbert Wertheim College of Engineering's Outstanding International Student Award. Read more: https://www.cise.ufl.edu/students-receive-outstanding-international-student-awards/
 
DEPARTMENT RANKING
Computer engineering graduate program ranks No. 16 among public institutions in the 2020 U.S. News & World Report list of best graduate programs. Read more: https://www.cise.ufl.edu/computer-engineering-graduate-program-ranks-no-16-among-public-institutions/
