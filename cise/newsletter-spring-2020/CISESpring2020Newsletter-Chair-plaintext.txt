MESSAGE FROM THE CHAIR

We are proud to share with you this summary of some of the news and events that have stood out in the last few months. Read more: https://www.cise.ufl.edu/spring2020-message-from-the-chair/

Juan E. Gilbert, Ph.D.
The Banks Family Preeminence Endowed Professor
CISE Department Chair
------------------------------------------------------------

VOTING TECHNOLOGY
Juan E. Gilbert, Ph.D., testified as an expert witness regarding election security during a hearing by the House Administration Committee in January. Read more: https://www.cise.ufl.edu/gilbert-testifies-before-u-s-house-committee-about-election-security/

CSAW COMPETITION
The UF Student InfoSec Team (UF-SIT) placed in several events at the CSAW Competition in November, a cybersecurity event held by the NYU Tandon School of Engineering. Read more: https://www.cise.ufl.edu/uf-sit-takes-first-in-pwny-race-at-nyus-csaw-competition/

UF ONLINE RANKING
UF Online, UF’s online undergraduate experience, has now risen to No. 4 in the country for best online bachelor’s programs according to U.S. News & World Report’s 2020 rankings. Read more: https://www.cise.ufl.edu/uf-online-ranked-no-4-among-nations-best-online-bachelors-programs/

CAREER AWARD
Sharon Lynn Chu, Ph.D., an assistant professor, receives an early career award from the National Science Foundation to investigate the design of wearable technologies. Read more: https://www.cise.ufl.edu/chu-receives-career-award-from-national-science-foundation/

AAAS FELLOW
Abdelsalam (Sumi) Helal, Ph.D., a professor, was named a 2019 Fellow of the American Association for the Advancement of Science (AAAS). Read more: https://www.cise.ufl.edu/helal-named-a-2019-aaas-fellow/

MENTORING AWARD
Kevin R. B. Butler, Ph.D., an associate professor, has been selected as a 2020 Herbert Wertheim College of Engineering Doctoral Dissertation Advisor/Mentoring Awardee. Read more: https://www.cise.ufl.edu/butler-selected-as-a-doctoral-dissertation-advisor-mentoring-awardee-by-college
