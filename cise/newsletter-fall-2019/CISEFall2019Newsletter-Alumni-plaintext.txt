DEPARTMENT NEWS
The University of Florida has been re-designated as a National Center of Academic Excellence in Cyber Defense Research (CAE-R) through academic year 2024. Read more: https://www.cise.ufl.edu/uf-designated-a-national-center-of-academic-excellence-in-cyber-defense-research/

INNOVATOR OF THE YEAR
Benjamin Lok, Ph.D., received the 2019 Innovator of the Year award from UF Innovate | Tech Licensing. Read more: https://www.cise.ufl.edu/lok-receives-innovator-of-the-year-award/

BERMUDEZ RETIRES
Manuel Bermudez, Ph.D., retired from the department after 34 years. Dr. Bermudez's research focused on programming languages, and he made several contributions in the area of syntax analysis in compilers. Read more: https://www.cise.ufl.edu/manuel-bermudez-retires-from-cise-after-34-years/

CISE CAREER FAIR
More than 1,200 students passed through check-in to meet with 37 companies at the Fall 2019 CISE Career Fair, formerly the Career Development Workshop. Read more: https://www.cise.ufl.edu/cise-career-fair-sees-largest-attendance/

CAREER AWARD
Kyla McMullen, Ph.D., earns an early career award from the National Science Foundation to enhance first responder effectiveness. Read more: https://www.cise.ufl.edu/mcmullen-uses-3d-audio-to-aid-firefighters-in-search-and-rescue-efforts/

RESEARCH SPOTLIGHT
Sanjay Ranka, Ph.D., was awarded $2 million from the NSF to develop technology that will monitor high-risk intersections. Read more: https://www.cise.ufl.edu/uf-engineer-leads-collaboration-for-safer-roadways/

MODERN FIGURES PODCAST
Kyla McMullen, Ph.D., and Jeremy A. Magruder Waisome, Ph.D., host the Modern Figures Podcast, which is sponsored by the National Center for Women & Information Technology. Read more: https://www.cise.ufl.edu/inspiring-the-next-generation-of-black-women-in-computing/
