UF President Kent Fuchs & Dean Cammy Abernathy invite you to an evening of camaraderie, conversation and celebration.

Oct. 22, 2015 • 6:00 p.m.
Rosewood Sand Hill • Menlo Park, CA

Hors d’oeuvres and drinks will be served.
Attire is business casual.

RSVP by October 15 to Kristen Carney at kcarney13@eng.ufl.edu
or call 352-392-6795.
This week, the UF College of Engineering launched The Wertheim Transformation, a $300M public-private partnership led by a $50M catalyst gift by Dr. Herbert and Nicole Wertheim and the Dr. Herbert & Nicole Wertheim Family Foundation.
The largest cash gift in the history of the University of Florida, this will launch the greatest expansion in the 105 year history of your College of Engineering, now the Herbert Wertheim College of Engineering.
UF President Kent Fuchs, himself an engineer, will join us on his first visit to the west coast on behalf of UF to discuss how the Wertheim Transformation will impact the future of the College and University of Florida as we move into national preeminence.
