UF President Kent Fuchs & Engineering Dean
Cammy Abernathy invite you to an evening of camaraderie and celebration of engineering innovator
Dr. Herbert Wertheim.
On October 1st, after receiving the largest cash gift in the history of the university, The University of Florida College of Engineering became the Herbert Wertheim College of Engineering. Come join President Fuchs, on his first visit to the west coast on behalf of UF, to honor Dr. Wertheim with an Innovation Award. Help us celebrate the $50M gift from Dr. Herbert and Nicole Wertheim and the Dr. Herbert & Nicole Wertheim Family Foundation which has catalyzed a $300M public-private investment.

Oct. 22, 2015 • 6-8 p.m.

Rosewood Sand Hill • 2825 Sand Hill Road • Menlo Park, CA

Hors d’oeuvres and drinks will be served
Business attire
