Online Engineering Master’s Degree

Expand your career opportunities with a master’s degree or certificate in engineering from a Top 10 public university.

The Herbert Wertheim College of Engineering at the University of Florida offers graduate degrees and certificates in engineering—completely online.

With the flexibility of EDGE (Electronic Delivery of Gator Engineering), you can power up your future with a leadership role in engineering on your own time.

Master’s degree tracks can be completed in just 24 months.

Twelve graduate certificates available in technical and leadership roles.

UF is No. 2 in the nation among public universities for best value. (Kiplinger Best College Values, 2018)

  • Environmental Engineering
  • Electrical & Computer Engineering
  • Industrial & Systems Engineering
  • Materials Science & Engineering
  • Mechanical & Aerospace Engineering
  • Engineering Leadership
  • Engineering Innovation
