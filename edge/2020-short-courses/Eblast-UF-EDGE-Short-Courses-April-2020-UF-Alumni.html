Professional Development - 100% Online

Gain the skills to effectively compete in today’s fast-moving innovation economy through our online Credential Builders for Engineers Short Course Program.
Credential Builders for Engineers are designed with working professionals in mind - they are available 100% online tailored to career advancement with a shorter time commitment than typical semester-based programs.

Students can expect weekly engagement with faculty.
